Sulzer Turbo Services Houston Inc.

For two summers, I worked as a student engineer at Sulzer. My work there involved a great deal of mechanical design, metals fabrication, and project management and was chiefly concerned with the repair and manufacture of large rotating equipment.

Department of Engineering Technology - Texas A&M Engineering

In my final semester at Texas A&M University, I worked as a teaching assistant for the Department of Engineering Technology's senior-level Rockwell Automation lab under its technical director, Professor Sheng-Jen ("Tony") Hsieh.

Engineering Innovation Center - Texas A&M Engineering

For three years while I studied at Texas A&M University, I worked at the Engineering Innovation Center. My work there consisted of project consultations for senior capstone groups and competition teams, and of the development and instruction of a number of machine shop classes.

Nanohmics, Inc.

In the summer of 2012, I worked in Austin, Texas for Nanohmics, Inc. There, I collaborated with scientists and other engineers to prototype high-intensity lighting equipment. My contributions included the design and fabrication of necessary components as well as the automation of test procedures.
